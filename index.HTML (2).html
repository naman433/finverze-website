The Finverze Cohort

7 weekends.
7 habits built for life.

Weekend 1
👁
See Your Money
Habit: Write down every rupee you spent this week. See it. Own it.
Weekend 2
⚖️
Needs vs Wants
Habit: Sort your last 10 spends live. Find your biggest money drain.
Weekend 3
🧠
Discipline
Habit: Spend ₹0 for 24 hours. Prove to yourself you control money — not the other way.
Weekend 4
🎯
Your First Goal
Habit: Set one savings goal. Calculate the monthly amount. Sign it. Done.
Weekend 5
📈
How Money Grows
Habit: Calculate what ₹500/month becomes at 40. Your number. Your future.
Weekend 6
💡
Myths Busted
Habit: Identify one money belief you had wrong. Replace it with a fact.
Weekend 7
🏆
Your Blueprint
Habit: Build your personal money plan. Sign it. Keep it. This one is for life.
